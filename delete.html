Delete My Account

PackStream by Erick — Melbourne, Australia

You can request deletion of your PackStream account and all associated data at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Deleting your account will remove all your saved packing lists and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

  • Your account credentials (email address)
  • All saved packing lists
  • All trip data associated with your account

What is not affected

  • Locally cached data on your device — uninstalling the app will remove this

How to request deletion

Send an email from the address associated with your PackStream account to:

📧 packstreamsuite@gmail.com

Request Account Deletion

Use the subject line Account Deletion Request and include the email address associated with your account. We will confirm deletion by reply email once complete.

Questions? Contact us at packstreamsuite@gmail.com
